Hampton Roads Super Grand Prix

A joint competition between the Colonial Road Runners, Peninsula Track Club, and Tidewater Striders, and held during Calendar year 2017, the Hampton Roads Super Grand Prix is designed to encourage participation in club running events throughout the entire metropolitan area and to offer expanded opportunities for competition.
